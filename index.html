2020 Census Response Rate

Map authored by Robert Early

The Decennial US Census is an important tool that affects representation in congress and allocation of federal funds.
Use your cursor to hover over a location and see how response rates (percent of distributed census forms that were completed and returned) vary accross counties.
More research should be done before the 2030 Census to identify possible reasons for low response rates and develop plans to address them.

Data courtesy US Census Bureau
